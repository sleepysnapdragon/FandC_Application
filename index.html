Something about me

On 17th December 1989 the very first episode of The Simpsons was broadcast. And over on the other side of the Atlantic, I was born.

A fairly uneventful childhood ensued. Protagonists included Penguin the guinea pig and XXX.

I then migrated north of the border to study English Literature, Language and Latin at the University of Edinburgh.

After university, in the absence of better ideas, I went to work in the London insurance market, where I learned about risk and financial modelling.

4 years later I faced up to the fact that the insurance industry wasn't really my thing, and turned down offers of an extensive sabbatical to quit outright and seek new pastures.

I had always wanted to live in a non-English speaking country and learn a new language, and in light of the Brexit referendum result, 2017 seemed like a good time to do that.

Off I went to Italy, Rome to be precise, with some suncream and a minor Duolingo-induced thumb sprain. Here I've spent two years teaching English (some English curiosity)
